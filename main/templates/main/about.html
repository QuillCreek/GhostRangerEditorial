The Ghost Ranger Editorial is a Pathfinder and Starfinder themed newspaper dedicated to the Pathfinder Society group located around the Huntsville, AL area. The Ghost Ranger Editorial is operated by Lacey A. Weeks, a member of the Pathfinder Society group. The name of the newspaper was inspired by one of her Pathfinder characters, an Ancient Elf Rogue with Ranger dedication named Ghost (also nicknamed "the Ghost Ranger").
